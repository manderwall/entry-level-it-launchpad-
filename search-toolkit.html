        How to verify a posting is legitimate

          Check a specific posting with your AI

          This site cannot verify a posting for you — it has no way to check a company's domain, look up other postings, or confirm a role is still open. What it can do is hand you a well-built prompt to paste the posting into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or whatever AI you already use, so it reasons over the red flags above systematically instead of you eyeballing it. Read the AI's answer as a second opinion, not a verdict.
