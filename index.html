Entry-Level IT Launchpad

Main message: Do not frame yourself as "no experience." Frame it as CompTIA A+ certified + Per Scholas trained + remote customer service experience = entry-level technical support candidate. The best first roles are people-facing tech jobs where communication matters as much as troubleshooting.
