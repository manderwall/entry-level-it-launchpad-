Resume bullets

These bullets are deliberately generic starting points — the single biggest thing you can do to make them stronger is add your own real numbers wherever you have them: tickets handled per day, average handle time, CSAT/satisfaction score, first- contact resolution rate, team size, anything from a performance review. A bullet like "resolved 40+ tickets/day at a 95% satisfaction score" beats the generic version every time — but only use numbers you can actually stand behind if asked about them in an interview.
