## Quick answer: best first targets

1. **Remote Technical Support Specialist** — $20-$25/hr, high remote odds
2. **Help Desk Analyst I / Service Desk Analyst** — $19-$25/hr, medium remote odds
3. **SaaS / Product Support Specialist** — $45K-$60K/yr, high remote odds
4. **Application Support Analyst / Support Analyst** — $55K-$70K/yr, medium-high remote odds
5. **IT Asset Coordinator / IT Asset Analyst** — $19-$32/hr, medium remote odds

## Salary floor and negotiation

Set the floor at **$19/hr**, but aim higher when possible.

- Minimum: $19/hr
- Realistic target: $20-$25/hr for help desk / technical support
- Strong target: $22-$28/hr for support analyst, NOC, application support, or better remote support
- Entry IT support salary: $45K-$55K
- Application support / support analyst / SaaS support salary: $55K-$70K+

**If asked for hourly expectations:** "For entry-level technical support
roles using my A+ certification and remote customer service background,
I'm targeting the $20-$25/hr range depending on schedule, training, and
benefits."

**If asked for salary expectations:** "I'm targeting the $45K-$55K range
for entry-level IT support, with flexibility depending on benefits,
growth path, and remote/hybrid structure."
