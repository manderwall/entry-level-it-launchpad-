# Employers, Pipelines & Apprenticeships

Structured data: [`data/employers.json`](../../data/employers.json).
Rendered on the [Employers page](../../employers.html).

## Staffing and contract-to-hire

Often the fastest path into first IT work: Robert Half Technology,
Kforce, TEKsystems, Apex Systems, Insight Global, Motion Recruitment,
Randstad Technologies, Experis / ManpowerGroup.

## Remote customer support / technical support pipelines

Not guaranteed openings, but useful to check repeatedly: TTEC,
Concentrix, Kelly / KellyConnect, Robert Half Technology, Kforce,
TEKsystems, Apex Systems / Insight Global / Experis. Watch pay on all of
these — some roles post below the $19/hr floor.

## Apprenticeships and paid training routes

[Apprenticeship.gov — Technology](https://www.apprenticeship.gov/apprenticeship-industries/technology)
lists tracks including Customer Service Tech Support, IT Generalist,
Network Support Technician, and Cybersecurity Support Technician. Recent
Houston-area IT Apprentice postings have paid around $26-$31/hr.

## Per Scholas-specific advantage

Per Scholas can be more than a line on the resume — use it as a referral
network. Ask career services which employers hired recent A+ graduates,
ask for introductions to TEKsystems/local MSPs/help-desk recruiters, ask
alumni where they got their first job, and ask for mock interviews
specifically for technical support.
