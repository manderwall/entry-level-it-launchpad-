## How to use this guide

1. Pick 2 primary targets and 1 backup target.
2. Search both remote and local/hybrid jobs. Fully remote entry-level IT
   is competitive; local/hybrid often converts faster.
3. Apply to roles that accept customer service + A+ + training instead
   of waiting for "perfect" postings.
4. Use Per Scholas career services and alumni referrals before
   cold-applying.
5. Do not pause the job search to study forever. Apply while learning.

## 2-week application plan

- **Days 1-2 — Prepare:** update resume headline, add remote
  customer-service bullets rewritten as support experience, set the
  LinkedIn headline, set job alerts.
- **Days 3-7 — Apply hard:** 8-12 jobs/day (4 remote technical/product
  support, 3 local/hybrid help desk or desktop support, 2 support
  analyst / application support, 1 IT asset or NOC).
- **Days 8-14 — Follow up and network:** message Per Scholas alumni and
  career staff, ask recruiters about contract-to-hire roles, follow up
  after 3-5 business days, practice the 60-second pitch.

## Weekly goals

Apply to 40-60 roles/week, message 5 recruiters/week, message 3 Per
Scholas alumni/week, complete 1 small technical project/week, do 2 mock
interviews/week, follow up after 3-5 business days.

## If money is tight while you search

Legitimate, free places to get help — Texas Workforce Commission
unemployment benefits, 211 Texas / United Way HelpLine, Workforce
Solutions / your local workforce board — listed on the Plan & Tracker
page with links.

**Sharing this with classmates?** Each person should duplicate their own
copy of the tracker (this site stores tracker data locally in your own
browser — nothing is uploaded or shared between visitors).
