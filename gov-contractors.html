How to use this list

  • This is sourced live from USAspending.gov, the federal government's own public contract-spending database — not a hiring board.
  • Contract size is a rough proxy for how much local staff the contractor might employ, not a guarantee of open roles.
  • Use the "Search openings" link on each company to check their actual careers page or job-board postings.
  • Confirm any company is actively registered to do business with the government at SAM.gov Entity Search before relying on it.
